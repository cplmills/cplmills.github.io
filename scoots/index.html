Welcome to Scoots Vehicle Hire

Scoots is possibly the best motor scooter rental company on the Island of Cozumel. Cozumel is located off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. Scoots are an owner-operated company that caters to locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships. Our company has two physical rental locations on Cozumel. One is near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other is near the Terminal Puerta Maya further south along the coast which caters to the cruise lines and patrons. We promote friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs.

We offer a wide range of vehicles starting from a single person scooter, all the way up to a 4-door Jeep Wrangler, perfect if you want to get off the beaten track.

With 2 easy to access locations on the island, we make it easy for you to rent and return wherever you like! Our first location is situated right by the Playa Del Carmen Cozumel Ferry Dock in El Centro De Cozumel. Our second location is near the terminal Puerta Maya, further south along the coast. Whether you want to explore the roads of the island on a scooter with the wind blowing in your hair, or go off road and explore our beaches and inland trails with a 4WD Jeep, we have the vehicle to suit your needs. Visit our Vehicles page for more information on the vehicles we have available to rent. From there, hop over to our Tours page for advise on where to visit once you have your wheels.
