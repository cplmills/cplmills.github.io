Weather Better

Doing weather... Better!

Target Audience

Our target audience at Weather Better are community members looking for a site to see events, check weather forecasts, and get involved in their local communities.

We wish to foster a sense of community, encouraging others to come together and socialise whilst giving visitors to an area,, visibility to community events being hosted around town.

Personas

Garry from Preston

Garry from Preston. Garry is a local business owner and is an avid networker. He has been in the car sales business for 20+ years and volunteers at his local shelters (animal and homeless) whenever the chance emerges.

Chris from England

Chris from England. Chris is from out of town, in fact he is from a different country! He is visiting his mum in Soda Springs for the first time. The pair are looking for things to do together in the area. They navigate to WeatherBetter.com.au and check out the list of events in Soda Springs.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Chris' Mum finds a community picnic that she would love to attend and thinks this will give her a good opportunity to introduce Chris to the people she has met in her community. Whilst there, they check the weather forecast. Its not much fun going to a picnic in the rain!

Scenario 2

Garry uses BetterWeather.com.au to find events that he could attend to introduce both himself, and his business to people in the area.

Scenario 3

Chris LOVES the weather channel on TV but cannot watch it when he is out and about as the audio annoys people on the bus and he cannot wear headphones. WeatherBetter.com.au gives Chris the ability to check on the weather in his are whilst on the go. It responds well to the size of his devices no matter which device he uses and he loves that he can get a quick glance on his phone, or a more detailed view on his iPad.

Scenario 4

Garry also uses the news shown on BetterWaether.com.au to see if there are any events that may indicate community members in need of support. He is passionate about supporting his community and is quick to help out when storms hit hard!

Scenario 5

Being on holiday, Chris has a lot of activities planned out for his trip. He quickly checks BetterWeather.com.au in the mornings whilst he has breakfast to ensure that the weather suitws the activities that he has planned.
