Site Purpose

Weather Better is a community site aimed at providing local communities a place to come to to connect. Site visitors will be able to monitor the current weather events in their area, as well as events that may be taking place in their community.

A gallery will provide images of recent or upcoming events in the community and will showcase the sence of belonging and community spirit that the site aims to foster.

Users will be able to check the weather in their area and correspond that to news and events that are taking place.

Requirements

  • The design is consistent throughout the website providing ease of navigation and supporting positive user experiences.
  • The header contains a logo, site title, and site motto.
  • The navigation menu contains links to the main pages including Home, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center, and Gallery.
  • The footer contains contact information and map in the upper section and copyright information, the author's name, a terms of use link, and the current date in the bottom row.
  • The home page contains a current weather story with corresponding image and information sections for all three towns or other content deemed appropriate by the student.
  • The town weather pages contain weather conditions and other town information or relevant articles/content.
  • Images are used to reflect the current weather condition data on the town pages and the towns themselves.
