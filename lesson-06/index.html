What's Happening In Your Area:

Preston - Picnic in the Park

Why not come along to the annual Preston Picnic in the Park and meet some new friends whilst we roll out the picnic blankets, sandwiches and soda. We will be located at meeting point 2, in the Preston National Park. Festivities kick off at 10am. There will be fairground rides for the kids, a jumping castle, and sporting games organised for the adults and big kids!

Last years picnic saw a huge turnout with over 200 people coming along to join the festivities. This year we are raising money for Olive Kids, a charity based in Sydney Australia, who help kids effected by war in the Gaza Strip.

Bring a plate of food to share and a blanket or two and enjoy the wonderful weather and great company of the people of Preston. Friends and family are welcome and we love to see people from out of town as well.
